Borrowing Guide

Steps for Borrowing:

  1. Work from right to left (ones to tens to hundreds)
  2. If top digit < bottom digit, borrow from the left
  3. Enter "1" in the borrow box above the column
  4. The borrowed 1 becomes 10 in that place
  5. Reduce the digit you borrowed from by 1
