Borrowing Guide

Steps for Borrowing:

  1. Work from right to left (ones to tens to hundreds)
  2. If top digit < bottom digit, borrow from the left
  3. Click the digit you want to borrow from
  4. That sends a small 1 to the digit on the right, making it 10 more
  5. The digit you borrowed from is reduced by 1
